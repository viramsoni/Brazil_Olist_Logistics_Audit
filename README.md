# Olist Brazil: Logistics & Loyalty Audit
This project analyzes 100k+ orders to uncover why customers don't return.

### 🚀 Key Discovery: The "Ghost Delivery" Fraud
- **System Reality**: 100% of orders are marked as "Delivered" in the database.
- **Human Reality**: 2.29% of customers left reviews stating "Não Recebi" (I did not receive it).
- **Financial Impact**: Over R$ 145,000 lost in refunds and trust, primarily in Rio de Janeiro (RJ).

### 💡 Conclusion
Customer loyalty is driven by **Product Category** (Consumables vs Durables), not **Delivery Speed**. Small delivery delays are less damaging than "Ghost Deliveries" where items are marked delivered but never arrive.
